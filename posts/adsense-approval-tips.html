1 Domain and Hosting :
Google AdSense requires a solid domain and hosting, which has a good domain age that gives them trust value.
in short, it doesn’t mean you buy a domain today and tomorrow you apply for AdSense — which is wrong.
so keep it in mind patience is everything, it may take you 2 years or 5 months for approval depending on your content.
if you use a custom built-in domain website like Blogger or free WordPress they don’t get monetized because Blogger has one issue which is unresolvable and free WordPress doesn’t let you monetize.
I have tried 1 year on all these so don’t waste your time.

2 Traffic :
even if you have a good website, good content but if you don’t have traffic all hard work is of no use because
AdSense doesn’t require any specific visits number but it may be needed for your earnings and the easy way is to share it on your social media accounts like Facebook, Pinterest, Quora, Twitter — they are popular for gaining views.

3 Patience :
You may receive rejections from AdSense but stay patient and fix the problems, because it takes time for everything.
so wait and give it some time.
